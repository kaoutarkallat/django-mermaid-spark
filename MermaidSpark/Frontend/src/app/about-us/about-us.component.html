Who We Are

Welcome to our MERMAID SPARK! We are a tech-driven online retailer located near Salt Lake City in the shadow of the Wasatch Mountains. Since our beginnings in 2012, MERMAID SPARK has evolved from a fledging startup to a million-dollar online retailer of sportwear as a result of a hard-working and creative team.

In our company, we highly value a healthy lifestyle and comfort. Our mission is to bring the latest fitness products to our loyal costumers.
